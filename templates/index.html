Meet The Team

The people behind Solaris

Arielle She/Her
Director

Hii, I'm Ari. As director, I focus on engineering the blueprint and long term vision for Solaris. I'm thoroughly detail oriented and analytical in making sure every part of the server is as fun and polished as possible for our players. It's been my passion to build Solaris, the flame of hope, that fills a gap that's been missing in this community for years.

Chaz Any Pronouns
Community Lead

Hiya :D I'm Chaz, the community lead manager here on Solaris! I help guide players, keep things safe, and also assist with map-building and a few background tasks. Outside of Solaris, you can find me playing Palia and Hello Kitty Island Adventure. I'll be online frequently around hub, playing games or flirting with Ari. So if you see me, don't be afraid to say hi!

Cookie She/They
Lead Builder

Hii I'm Cookie, I am the Lead Builder of Solaris. I do the building of maps for games, minigames and other things like the spawn. I enjoy working on building interiors the most, I also really enjoy doing Java Blockbench models and physical painting like watercolours or sketching. You'll usually find me building, snacking on food or streaming on Twitch!

Skye She/Her
Art Director

Hiya, I'm Skye! I'm part of the team working on Solaris. I mainly focus on the visual and creative side of the server, from art direction and building to UI and web design. I also spend a lot of time working on game design ideas and refining features alongside the team. If you see me in-game or in the Discord, feel free to say hi!

Nate He/Him
Manager

Hi, I'm Nate. I do a mix of things but usually I'm thinking of new ideas for cosmetics, games, and stuff for our players to interact with, like having VR working as well as most other mods that improve social life on the server. In my free time I'm usually donating my time to a FIRST Robotics team, feel free to reach out if you want more info.
