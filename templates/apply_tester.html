Before you apply

Please read the rules carefully. Testers are trusted members of our community and we take this seriously.

No leaking. Do not share anything that isn't publicly known, especially in the main community Discord.

No harassment or bullying. Treat every member of the team and testing group with respect.

No NSFW content. Keep all channels and communications appropriate.

Recording & streaming. You may livestream and record for bug-finding purposes unless we announce otherwise. Watch the announcements channel.

No offensive humour. Do not make hurtful jokes about serious topics.

Blacklist agreement. If accepted, you will be blacklisted from the live event for at least 2 testing cycles after you last played.

You must agree to all rules and own a Java account to apply as a tester.
