# ARCHIVE - Development Update & Early Access Pause

As development expanded, we announced that public updates would become less frequent for a while so the team could focus on substantial progress rather than small weekly reveals.

To reduce pressure and keep work sustainable, Early Access was temporarily removed from the store. Existing supporters retained their access, and Early Access was planned to return closer to release.

This period set the tone for a quieter, build-first phase focused on long-term quality.
