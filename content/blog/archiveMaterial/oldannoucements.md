Some of you may have already noticed, Clubmine is being rebranded to Solaris. Some reasoning is outlined below, but most questions can be asked in social or clarified in our owner call at the end of this month. 😄 

Our reasoning for the change comes from the increasing likelihood of complex legal issues surrounding the intention behind the name Clubmine. We also do not want to cause any confusion among Mineclub's playerbase & our own. Not only are we protecting ourselves, we are also developing a more nuanced identity. When we originally decided the name we were not serious about making a real Minecraft server. This has changed over the last year. We will thrive much more as Solaris.

There is also a stigma that comes with just having the name Clubmine, many people distance themselves because we are passed off as just an offbrand Mineclub, and while we are taking advantage of the fact that many of Mineclub's ideas have gone unexplored in other servers, we are still our own entity and not at all associated. We also want to avoid any further potential confusion for our playerbase & Mineclub's playerbase. These are not the same servers, nor are we the same people. 

I want to also note that in the last year I've personally learned a lot & it was foolish to create this Discord community & promise something we weren't entirely knowledgeable about in advance. Our original vision to create a better Mineclub out of love for those memories and unique experiences we shared has been so much more monumental of a task than we could have ever anticipated. This type of server though unfortunately just lacks popularity & 90%+ of the community is no longer here. While we've made a lot of progress in the last year, we are not satisfied with where we are. We set the goalpost to Summer 2024, then to January, then Q1 2025. We're in a difficult spot, and a lot of troubles have come up along the way, not just in developing Solaris, legally, and personally as well for a few of us on the team. At this point, we've put so much time, energy, and love and pure passion, as well as a fair chunk of money into this that we will not just abandon it, we can't. It's just unfortunate how much of a struggle it is to keep the lights on (continuous development) when we haven't even released. It's stressful & I really wasn't prepared a year ago for any of this. Regardless, we will continue developing at our own pace. This is a passion / side-project & it would be unsustainable for us to keep pouring more and more in when we have so much we have to do outside of Solaris. I hope as we navigate our situation, you all will be there when we release.

This should not come as a surprise to anyone considering that people are still treating us as a joke in the community who expected we were just going to be Mineclub 2 🤣 . While that is where a lot of our motivation comes from, we want to develop our own identity. We are thankful for the support you all have given us so far & we hope as we continue to develop and create that you all will want to support us even more.
