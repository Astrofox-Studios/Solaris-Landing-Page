# Building the Hub World

Hey everyone, Cookie here! I wanted to give you a peek at how the hub world is coming together.

## The Vision

The hub is the first thing players see when they join Solaris, so it needs to feel special. We wanted something that's both impressive and cosy — a place you'd actually want to hang out in, not just a menu lobby.

## Interior Details

I've been spending a lot of time on the interiors — little shops, seating areas, and hidden spots to explore. Every corner has something to discover.

## What's Next

There's still a lot of detailing left to do, but the main structure is in place. Can't wait for you all to see it in person!
