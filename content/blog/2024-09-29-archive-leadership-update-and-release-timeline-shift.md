# ARCHIVE - Leadership Update & Release Timeline Shift

In this period, we announced internal team changes, including ownership updates aimed at improving execution and long-term consistency.

We also shared that beta would move further out than originally expected. While difficult, the delay reflected a commitment to shipping in a stronger state rather than forcing a deadline.

This was one of the clearest moments where the project prioritized sustainability and quality over short-term optics.
