# ARCHIVE - Community Kickoff & First Owner Call

In our first few days as a community, growth moved faster than we expected, with hundreds of members joining early and helping spread the word.

We hosted our first owner call to explain the vision behind the project, answer questions directly, and set expectations for what came next.

Following that call, we temporarily restricted public server access and enabled whitelist-only development while we prepared for beta.

This marked the transition from early hype into focused behind-the-scenes building.
