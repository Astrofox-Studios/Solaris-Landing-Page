# Welcome to Solaris!

We're thrilled to announce the launch of **Solaris** — a brand new Minecraft server experience brought to you by The Solaris Team.

## What is Solaris?

Solaris is our vision for a Minecraft community where players can explore, compete, and create together. Whether you're a seasoned builder, a PvP enthusiast, or just looking for a chill place to hang out with friends — there's something here for everyone.

## How to Join

Connecting is simple! Just add `playsolaris.net` to your Minecraft server list and jump right in.

## Stay Connected

Follow us on our socials and join our Discord to stay up to date with the latest news, events, and updates. We can't wait to see you in-game!
