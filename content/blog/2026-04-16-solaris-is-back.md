# Welcome Back to Solaris

Hey everyone,

You have probably seen small teasers lately, and we can finally say it clearly: **Solaris is back**.

We have been building behind the scenes for a while now. A lot has changed, a lot has improved, and we are excited to start showing more of what we have been working on. We still have key milestones to finish first, but the wait is almost over.

## Website Is Live

Our official website is now live and it is the best place to follow everything Solaris:

- Team and project information
- Community links and updates
- Future announcements and rollout news

If you have been waiting for a central place to stay in sync with us, this is it.

## Early Access Signups

Early Access signups are now open. Signups include a chance to receive Early Access **for free** when rollout begins in the coming months.

This helps us bring in active players as we prepare each phase and gather feedback before wider access.

## We Are Hiring Pixel Artists

We are actively looking for **Pixel Artists** who specialize in **UI Art**.

If that sounds like your lane and you want to help shape Solaris visually, check the application form on the website.

Thanks for sticking with us. More reveals are coming soon.
