# Solaris Competitive Vision

Solaris is being built as a social-first server with real competitive depth.

Our goal is simple: matches should feel fun in the moment, and progression should still matter over time.

## Core Principles

- **Skill matters**: decisions, teamwork, and consistency should win games.
- **Fair systems**: competitive outcomes should be earned in-game.
- **Replay value**: modes should stay fresh and rewarding over time.

## What This Means in Practice

We are designing game systems around clarity and player agency:

- Strong matchmaking and event flow
- Reward structures that support active play
- Cosmetics and progression that celebrate participation and achievement

We are also focused on making Solaris a place where players can grow from casual sessions into high-level competition without losing the fun that made them join.

## Long-Term Direction

As Solaris grows, competitive experiences will expand with new events, balance updates, and more opportunities for players and communities to stand out.

This is not just about one launch moment. It is about building a competitive ecosystem that lasts.
