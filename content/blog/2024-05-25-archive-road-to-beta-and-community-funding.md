# ARCHIVE - Road to Beta & Community Funding

This update outlined the scale of our plans: competitive modes, social systems, cosmetics, and a long-term vision that could eventually include larger persistent experiences.

We also confirmed that maintaining development had become expensive, and launched Patreon support to help fund infrastructure and ongoing work.

The message was clear: release would be built around quality and depth, not speed.
