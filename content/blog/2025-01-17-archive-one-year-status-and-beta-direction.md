# ARCHIVE - One-Year Status & Beta Direction

One year in, we shared a candid recap of where Solaris stood: core systems were being recoded for performance and maintainability, and old Clubmine branding had been fully phased out.

We also outlined the beta direction, including unobtainable event rewards, cosmetic progression, and the first game rollout plan.

This post served as both a milestone reflection and a clearer view into how launch-era Solaris would feel to players.
