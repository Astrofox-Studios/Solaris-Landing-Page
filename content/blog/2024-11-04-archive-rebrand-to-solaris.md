# ARCHIVE - Rebrand to Solaris

This announcement marked the formal rebrand from Clubmine to Solaris.

The change was driven by legal and identity concerns: we wanted to avoid confusion with other communities while building a distinct long-term brand.

We also confirmed the transition to Solaris domains and server identity, closing the chapter on legacy naming.

The rebrand became a foundational milestone for everything that followed.
